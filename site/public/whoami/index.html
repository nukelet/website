About me

I’m Vinicius, a 22-year-old Computer Engineering student from Brazil.

I enjoy tinkering with computers and making them break in funny ways. My main interests in CS are operating systems, computer architectures (particularly RISC-V), parallel computing and computer graphics. I’m currently getting my feet wet with contributing to the Linux kernel.

Other than that, I’m a huge fan of Linguistics, particularly typology. Language learning has been one of my favorite activities for a long time, and I’m currently struggling with learning Mandarin Chinese. I’m also an avid music listener and enjoy dabbling in bass and brazilian guitar in my free time.
